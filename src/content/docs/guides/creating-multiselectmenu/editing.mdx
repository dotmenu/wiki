---
title: Editing Options
---

To modify options, use `menu.options[index].Action()`.
Replace "menu" with your menu's name and "index" with the option's index.
"Action" can be replaced with SetText - to replace the text  or SetHidden - to hide or unhide the option.
You can also use GetText - to get the text and GetHidden - to check if an option is hidden.
