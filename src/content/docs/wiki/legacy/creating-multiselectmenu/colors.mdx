---
title: Colors
---

You can customize the foreground and background colors using the `Colors` method.

```csharp
menu.Color(OptionColor.Yellow, OptionColor.Blue);
menu.ColorsWhenSelected(OptionColor.Blue, OptionColor.Yellow);
```

Or customize them for specific options:

```cs
menu.AddOption(..., fg: OptionColor.White, bg: OptionColor.Black, selectedFg: OptionColor.Black, selectedBg: OptionColor.White);
```
